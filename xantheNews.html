Announcing the Death of Xanthe ███ Miller

By the President of the United States of America

A Proclamation

To the People of the United States:

It is my sad duty to announce officially the death of Xanthe ███ Miller, the 49th President of the United States. She has been taken from us by an attack that shocked and angered the entire nation yesterday, June 3, 2036.

God chose her — a leader of wisdom and strength — to guide our nation through her vision and ideals. She was, to our Nation what the sun is to the Earth: her light reached and warmed every soul who belong to this land. As she once said, “This is the best age, the age of the people.” With a heart that ever burned with devotion to her citizens, she served a world that belongs to all, and labored to make it ever more free and orderly.

Now, Therefore, I, ███ █ ███, President of the United States of America, in honor and tribute to the memory of Xanthe ███ Miller, and as an expression of public sorrow, do appoint hereby June 5, the day of the funeral service of President Miller, to be a National Day of Mourning throughout the United States. I invite the people of the world who share our grief to join us on this day of mourning and rededication.

In Witness Whereof, I have hereunto set my hand and caused the Seal of the United States of America to be affixed.

DONE at the City of Washington this fourth day of June in the year of our Lord two thousand and thirty-six, and of the Independence of the United States of America the two hundred and sixty.
